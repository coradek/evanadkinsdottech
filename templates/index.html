About Me

Evan Adkins Data Scientist

Data Science:
Data science provides an endless array of challenging and fascinating problems. I enjoy the process of developing simple concepts into complex techniques capable of drawing subtle interactions from numbers found in the real world. My favorite challenges, of course, are those with the potential to have significant positive impact for the people and organizations involved: check out my on going project with Conservation Northwest!

Mathematics and Education:
I am also a Math Instructor, teaching individuals and small groups everything from elementary to graduate level mathematics.

Ecology and Citizen Science:
When I am not roaming the world of numbers and mathematical abstraction, I can often be found roaming the Cascades. For the past four years I have been a Team Leader with the Cascade Citizen Wildlife Monitoring Project, tracking wildlife along the I-90 corridor in partnership with the Department of Transportation. Whether snow shoeing with my trusty data-dog Zephyr, tracking wildlife for a local conservation group, or just taking a hike, the outdoors are my second home.
